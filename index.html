Dual Subtitle Video Player

This video player allows you to load two subtitle files and switch between them using the '.' (dot) key. It is excellent for learning languages. Works with Chrome and Firefox, although Firefox only changes track when the next caption is loaded (Chrome changes immediately).

Usage:

  1. Pick two subtitle files in .vtt format.
  2. Pick a video.
  3. Enjoy!
Remark: Nothing is uploaded anywhere; the video plays locally from your browser.
